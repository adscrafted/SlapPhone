Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

SlapPhone ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use SlapPhone.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

SlapPhone operates entirely on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or files
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share any data with third parties

Device Permissions

SlapPhone requests access to:

  • Motion & Accelerometer: Used solely to detect physical impacts (slaps, shakes, throws) on your device. This data is processed locally and never leaves your device.

Local Data Storage

SlapPhone stores the following data locally on your device using UserDefaults:

  • Your app preferences (sensitivity settings, selected voice pack)
  • Statistics (slap count, throw count, etc.)
  • Purchase status for the Full Version

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

In-App Purchases

SlapPhone offers a one-time In-App Purchase processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple.

Children's Privacy

SlapPhone does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
