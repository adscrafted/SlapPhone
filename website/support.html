Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use SlapPhone?

Simply open the app, tap "Start Detection", and slap, shake, or throw your phone. It will react with voice sounds based on the intensity of the impact.

Why isn't my phone detecting slaps?

Try adjusting the sensitivity slider in Settings. If it's set too high, light impacts may not register. Also ensure you've granted motion sensor permissions.

How do I unlock all voice packs?

Tap on "Voice Packs" and select "Unlock All" to purchase the Full Version. This is a one-time purchase with no subscriptions.

Does SlapPhone work in the background?

Yes! SlapPhone can detect impacts even when minimized. Enable "Background Mode" in Settings to keep detection running.

Will slapping my phone damage it?

SlapPhone is designed to work with normal handling. We recommend using reasonable force - your phone's accelerometer is sensitive enough to detect light taps. Please don't throw your phone at walls!

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
