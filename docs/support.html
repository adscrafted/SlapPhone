Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my phone detecting slaps?

Make sure detection is enabled (green dot in the top-left). Also check that the sensitivity slider isn't set too low. Try increasing sensitivity in Settings if light slaps aren't registering.

Can I use SlapPhone in the background?

Yes! SlapPhone continues detecting impacts when minimized. Make sure "Background Mode" is enabled in Settings. Note: Background detection uses more battery.

How do I change voice packs?

Tap the voice pack indicator in the top-right of the home screen, or tap the waveform icon at the bottom. Select any of the 4 included voice packs.

Why does my phone vibrate with every slap?

That's haptic feedback! It makes impacts feel more satisfying. You can disable it in Settings if you prefer sounds only.

What's the USB Moaner feature?

When you plug in or unplug your charger, SlapPhone plays a reaction sound. Your phone has feelings about being plugged in. You can disable this in Settings.

Will slapping my phone damage it?

SlapPhone detects impacts using the accelerometer - no actual violence required. A firm tap or shake works great. That said, we take no responsibility if you get too enthusiastic. Please slap responsibly.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

SlapPhone is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID. On a new device, just download the app and it should recognize your purchase automatically.

How do I reset my statistics?

Go to Settings and scroll to the bottom for the "Reset Statistics" option. This clears your lifetime slaps, throws, and shakes. This action cannot be undone.

Pro Tip: For the best experience, try different voice packs in different situations. "Dramatic" is great for parties, "Silly" is perfect for kids, and "Angry" is excellent for stress relief.
