Privacy Policy

SlapPhone - Last updated: March 2026

TL;DR: SlapPhone doesn't collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

SlapPhone ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your data belongs to you, and we've built our app to respect that.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The App does not:

  • Collect or store personal information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or files
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use advertising identifiers

Device Sensors

SlapPhone uses your device's motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope) to detect impacts, shakes, and throws. This sensor data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never stored beyond the current session
  • Never transmitted anywhere
  • Used only to trigger audio and haptic responses

Local Statistics

The App tracks fun statistics like "lifetime slaps" and "hardest hit." This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never uploaded or synced
  • Deleted if you uninstall the App

Purchases

SlapPhone is a one-time purchase with no subscriptions. Your purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

SlapPhone does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. There are no ads, no tracking pixels, and no data brokers.

Children's Privacy

SlapPhone does not collect data from anyone, including children. The App is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to secure on our end. Your local statistics are stored using iOS's standard secure storage mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Since we don't collect data, significant changes are unlikely.
