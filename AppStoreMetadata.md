## Description

**YOUR PHONE HAS HAD ENOUGH.**

SlapPhone turns your iPhone into a screaming, dramatic, attention-seeking device that reacts to every slap, shake, and throw. Go ahead - hit your phone. It'll let you know how it feels about that.

**FEATURES:**

• **SLAP DETECTION** - Uses your phone's accelerometer to detect impacts. Light tap? Gentle "ow." Full slap? Dramatic scream.

• **THROW DETECTION** - Toss your phone in the air and catch it. SlapPhone knows when it's flying and reacts accordingly.

• **SHAKE DETECTION** - Shake your phone and hear it protest.

• **USB MOANER** - Plug in your charger and... well, your phone has feelings about that too.

• **4 VOICE PACKS:**
  - Default: Classic "ow", "ouch", and grunts
  - Angry: Frustrated yelling and growls
  - Dramatic: Over-the-top theatrical screams
  - Silly: Cartoon boings, squeaks, and honks

• **ADJUSTABLE SENSITIVITY** - Set how hard you need to hit for a reaction

• **LIFETIME STATS** - Track your total slaps, throws, shakes, and your hardest hit ever

• **HAPTIC FEEDBACK** - Feel the impact with vibration responses

• **BACKGROUND MODE** - Works even when minimized

**WHY SLAPPHONE?**

Because sometimes you just need to hit something. And sometimes that something should scream back. SlapPhone is the stress relief app you didn't know you needed.

Perfect for:
- Stress relief
- Party entertainment
- Pranking friends
- Making boring moments chaotic
- Answering the age-old question: "What if my phone had feelings?"

**ONE-TIME PURCHASE. NO ADS. NO SUBSCRIPTIONS.**

Download SlapPhone and give your phone something to complain about.
